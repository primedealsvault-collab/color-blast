🎮 How To Play
Color Blast — Quick Guide
🎯 The Basics
👁️
A colored ball appears at the top of the screen.
👆
Tap the button below that matches the ball's color — before the timer runs out!
⏱️
The timer bar at the top drains fast. Tap quickly or you lose a life!
💰 Scoring & Coins
✅
Each correct tap = +10 points and coins.
🔥
Tap correctly in a row to build a Combo — get up to +40 points per tap!
🪙
Coins are earned every tap. Spend them in the Shop to buy power-ups and themes.
❤️ Lives
💔
You start with 3 lives. You lose one if you tap the wrong color or the timer runs out.
💀
When all lives are gone — Game Over!
⚡ Power-Ups
💡
Hint — Tap to make the correct button glow for 2 seconds.
❄️
Freeze — Tap to stop the timer for 5 seconds.
🛡️
Shield — Auto-blocks one wrong tap. You won't lose a life!
✖️
2x Score — Doubles all points for one full round.
🎮 Game Modes
♾️
Endless — Play as long as you can. Standard coins.
⏱️
Time Attack — Score as much as possible in 60 seconds. Earn 2x coins!
💀
Hardcore — Only 1 life, super fast timer. But earn 3x coins!
🌀
CHAOS Mode — Colors change mid-round, fake trap buttons appear, buttons shuffle, and the timer randomly speeds up! Earn 4x coins if you survive!
⬆️ Leveling Up
📈
As your score grows, you level up — the timer gets faster and more color buttons appear!
🎁
Come back every day to claim a Daily Reward — coins increase with your streak!
